Final work for the chair of operational research of the third year of the systems career, of the national university of the center of the province of Buenos Aires.
It is an application of game theories, oriented to online poker, using the Nash equilibrium, or GTO.
During its development it was necessary to use all the main concepts of OOP: isomorphism, polymorphism, multiple inheritances, compositions, filters, main patterns, etc.
Was necesary to use a scripting, trough autohotkey software.
